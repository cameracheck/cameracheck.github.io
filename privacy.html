Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

At CameraCheck, we believe your privacy is non-negotiable. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our camera testing tools.

1. Zero Data Collection

CameraCheck is designed to be a "Zero-Footprint" utility. We do not collect, store, or transmit any video or audio data from your camera or microphone to our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser[cite: 1, 2].

2. Browser Permissions

To provide diagnostic information, our tool requires temporary access to your media devices. This permission is scoped only to the current tab and can be revoked at any time through your browser settings[cite: 2].

3. Local Processing

Diagnostics such as Resolution, FPS, and Aspect Ratio are extracted using the WebRTC and MediaDevices APIs entirely client-side[cite: 2]. We do not use persistent cookies for tracking or profiling purposes.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites.

5. Security

We enforce HTTPS to ensure that the communication between your browser and our tool is encrypted, preventing unauthorized interception of your hardware stream[cite: 2].
